Introduction

Bollywood Brow

Bollywood Brow is a waxing, threading, and facial salon in the beauty service industry specializing in traditional Indian beauty practices.

Target audience

The target audience is consumers looking to feel beautiful before big events, especially Brown females. Their goal is to find a credible salon that feels welcoming and inviting, since the procedures can be scary. They also want transparency and need clear descriptions of what exactly goes on with each service.

Their primary tasks include browsing services and prices to see what fits their needs the most, noting down hours, and finding a phone number to call.

ABOUT

Established in 2018 by an all-female Indian aesthetician team with years of professional experience, Bollywood Brow is the San Jose’s newest premier salon, happily serving customers from all backgrounds across the Bay Area. We utilize Ayurvedic medicinal ingredients and hair removal methods grounded in tradition passed on by centuries of Brown women. At Bollywood Brow, we strive to empower our customers, bringing out the beauty you have always had, and most of all, helping you feel the most confident in your own skin. Walk in to schedule your free consultation or book an appointment with us by phone today!
